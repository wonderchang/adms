Topic 1: Sampling Theorem

Exercise 1

Consider a cosine signal with 2π period. Please sample it with different frequency and discuss the results to demonstrate why Sampling Theorem works.

Because the cosine is a very pure periodic signal, we can easily identify its frequency domain, remaining only two impules at the -1/2π and 1/2π. So the maxima frequency of a cosine wave with 2π period is 1/2π. According to Sampling Theorem, the sampling frequency should be two times greater than the maxima frequency of the signal, which means the sampling frequency should greater than 1/π (fs > 1/π).
